About Me

Who am I?

I'm Stefan Nikolaj, a 16 year old student from Macedonia. I like programming, video games, electronics, history and many other things. But I'm interested in many things, and know a lot about weird areas like retro computers, the history of pop music, synthesizers, development of tanks and other armored vehicles, 6th century European history and many other things.

I have used all 3 major operating systems, and currently use both Manjaro Linux and Windows 10. Though the Manjaro Linux might change, because I change my distros somewhat often. I love C++, but have made interesting things in every programming language. The Java Processing framework in particular is one I love. BASIC is also an awesome language. Recently I've also been doing things with Arduinos and microcontrollers and I love it. I've also been looking to buy some retro computers, so if you're in the Balkans and have a retro computer (especially domestic Yugoslav ones), please contact me.

I don't have one favorite video game, but there are many I love. More notable games I love are World of Tanks, the Command and Conquer series, the Total War series, the Men of War series, the Fallout series and many more.
