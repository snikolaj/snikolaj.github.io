The three Rs of recycling

This will be a new series where I how you what you can do with broken or unneeded electronics. Leaving unneeded electronics somewhere in a basement is a waste of space, and throwing them away is extremely harmful to the  environment. There will be 3 blog posts where I show you what to do to recycle a particular electronic device. Repairing electronics is has been my hobby for a couple of months now, and I couldn’t be happier with it.
